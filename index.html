You've found your home! Join us for games, strategy and a fun environment to share the love of the game in a friendly space. We're an online cohort of AoE 2 enthusiasts, united by our passion for the game and our desire to have a great time together. ## About our group We're a group of dedicated Age of Empires II players with an Elo rating typically between 700 and 1300. Founded from encountering fun and skilled players on the ladder, our group has grown through invites slowly over time. We are here to have fun playing the game together with this great community and are built from helping each other improve! Team games are regularly played on all nights across a wide range of time zones, so there is always someone on to play with, and players use each other to practice 1v1 strategies for RM or Tournaments. ## What We Do Here's what you can expect: - **Team Games:** We frequently organize and play team games over voice, bringing strategy and cooperation to the forefront. - **1v1 Practice:** Looking to sharpen your individual skills? We regularly practice 1v1 matches, offering a supportive environment for improvement. - **Community Games:** Beyond ranked play, we enjoy various community games, fostering camaraderie and diverse gameplay experiences. - **Climbing the Ladder:** We actively help each other climb higher in the ranked ladder, sharing tips, strategies, and encouragement. Our group has seen members rise from 900 elo to 1100 over the course of several months from consistent participation. - **Learn the Game:** One of the best aspects of this group is being able to teach new players the fundamentals of how to succeed in having fun, which also leads to winning! - **Friendly and welcoming environment:** Age Buddies is first and foremost a place for friendly discussion and banter. While the game gets frustrating at times, we respect the player behind the game. ## Join Our Community! Ready to jump into the action?
